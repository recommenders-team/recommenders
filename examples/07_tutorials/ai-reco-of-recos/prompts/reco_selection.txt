**Selection Rules (compressed)**

* *Implicit + ids\_only* → BPR (pairwise), SAR (similarity), LightGCN (graph), NCF (deep).

* *Real-time bandit / low\_latency* → pre-compute candidates + VW or LightGBM re-rank.
